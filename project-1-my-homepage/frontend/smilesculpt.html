Aesthetic Controls

Generative Inpainting

Traditional models struggle with dental structures, often producing blurry texturing or irregular teeth counts. SmileSculpt utilizes high-precision localized mask guidelines combined with Stable Diffusion XL (SDXL) and custom ControlNet constraints. This locks spatial tooth configurations in place, enabling lifelike shading, matching textures, and realistic occlusion layers.

Clinical ControlNet Bounds

To prevent the model from generating anatomically impossible mouth sizes or shape bounds, custom depth maps and canny edge matrices are calculated from original patient structures. These bounds act as strict structural locks, forcing the generative diffusion model to focus exclusively on texture, brightness adjustment, and micro-alignment variables.
